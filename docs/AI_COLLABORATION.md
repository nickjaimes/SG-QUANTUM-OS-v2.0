### 1) Bidirectional Understanding
Human → expresses goals  
AI → proposes architecture & solutions

### 2) Constant Knowledge Access
No more:
- "Who knows this subsystem?"
- "Where is documentation?"

AI can reconstruct + explain context anytime.

### 3) Amplified Iteration
More experiments per hour → better designs.
